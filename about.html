Our History
Since 1985, Vitello di Lusso has brought the essence of Italy to our town. Founded by the Rossi family, our restaurant celebrates authentic Italian cuisine, inspired by Nonna Maria's Tuscan recipes. We pride ourselves on using the finest ingredients, from locally-sourced produce to premium imported Italian delicacies. Our signature dish, the Vitello di Lusso sirloin steak, is a testament to our commitment to quality and tradition. Served in a cozy, rustic setting reminiscent of an Italian countryside trattoria, we invite you to join us and be part of our family tradition. Grazie mille!
To maintain the highest standards of quality and taste, We focus on continually refining our techniques and exploring new flavors to offer our guests the very best of Italy. Join us in our cozy, rustic setting where we celebrate the timeless charm of Italian dining!
